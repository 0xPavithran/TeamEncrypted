CRYPTO CURRENCY SCAMS AND HOW TO PREVENT THEM:

A cryptocurrency scam refers to fraudulent activities
in the crypto space where scammers deceive others to
gain unauthorized access to funds or sensitive information.
Scammers exploit the decentralized and pseudonymous nature
of cryptocurrencies to trick individuals into investing or
sharing private information. To avoid falling victim, stay
informed, verify sources, use secure wallets, and exercise
skepticism towards high-return promises. Always prioritize
security and conduct due diligence before engaging in any
crypto-related transactions.

SILK ROAD:

The Silk Road was an online black market where users could
buy and sell illicit goods anonymously. It operated via
darknets, anonymous networks that can only be accessed
through specialized software like Tor Browser. Silk Road
was the first modern dark web marketplace. Accessed with
an app, the Tor network allowed users to anonymously
browse Silk Road without the threat of traffic monitoring

DARK WEB:

The dark web is a hidden part of the internet that
requires specific software, like Tor, to access. It
hosts websites anonymously, making user identities
and locations hard to trace. While it provides
privacy for legitimate purposes, it is also a space
where illegal activities, such as illicit trade,
hacking services, and more,occur.Caution is advised
when navigating the dark web due to the potential
for encountering criminal elements and engaging in
unlawful transactions.
